# Plend Local Pricing Benchmark

## Purpose
Estimate realistic local price anchors for Plend using nearby Barcelona/Castelldefels/Gavà market signals.

## Important note
This is a **directional pricing benchmark**, not a perfect live catalog of every competitor.
But it is enough to sharpen Plend pricing instead of guessing blindly.

---

# 1. Evidence gathered

## A. At-home workshop pricing in Barcelona
Talleres Infantiles BCN lists at-home workshop packs at:
- **€110** for up to 6 children
- **€130** for up to 8 children
- **€150** for up to 10 children
- plus **€30 transport** outside Barcelona city

This is useful because it shows the lower-mid market anchor for simple mobile children’s activity services.

## B. Decoration pricing in Barcelona
Cumpleparty lists decoration packages from:
- **€250 + VAT** for a small balloon/decor package
- **€450 + VAT** for a more complete setup with structure and personalized print
- **€690 + VAT** for a larger decoration package
- smaller balloon bouquet offer from **€159 + VAT**

This is a very useful anchor for premium-ish decoration pricing.

## C. Animation competitors in Castelldefels
Local competitors like Chispitas, Jajejijoju, and Mimos y Monas clearly sell:
- home service
- setup/transport/staffing
- personalized quotes

But most do not show exact prices on the fetched pages.
That usually means one of two things:
- they price by quote and customization
- they want to avoid easy comparison on price alone

## D. Venue alternative in Gavà
Jungla Park in Gavà signals strong competition from the “just book a venue birthday” option.
That means Plend pricing cannot drift too high without a clear premium-home value story.

---

# 2. What these anchors mean

## Cheap activity market floor
A basic mobile kids activity can exist around:
- **€110 to €180**

That is **not** Plend’s lane.
That is closer to a simple workshop or animator offer.

## Decor market anchor
A visually meaningful decor package in Barcelona already sits around:
- **€250 to €690 + VAT**

That means Plend should not be shy about charging for decor if:
- it looks polished
- setup is included
- the result is photo-worthy

## Full-service opportunity
If simple activity offers start near €110 to €180, and decent decor starts near €250+, then a combined premium under-6 package can credibly live in the:
- **€450 to €900** range

That matches the earlier model pretty well.

# 3. Recommended Plend pricing ladder

## Entry / lead offer
### Mini Deco
- **€220 to €290**

Why:
- stays close enough to visible decor market entry
- still protects against looking cheap

## Play-only offer
### Mini Play
- **€280 to €420**

Why:
- should sit above simple activity pricing because it includes physical equipment, logistics, setup, and cleanup

## Main offer
### Signature Plend
- **€520 to €750**

Why:
- this is the right zone if combining decor + play + setup + supervision
- still believable in the target zones if the visual quality is there

## Premium offer
### Premium Plend
- **€850 to €1,300+**

Why:
- consistent with upscale decor/event pricing once play, styling, and managed execution are layered in

---

# 4. How not to screw this up

## Do not anchor to cheap animators
If you compare yourself only to cheap animation or workshop pricing, you will underprice badly.

## Do not price based only on materials
You are selling:
- delivery
- setup labor
- aesthetic curation
- supervision
- teardown
- asset reset

## Do not hide the value logic
Customers need to understand that this is:
- beautiful
- safe
- managed
- convenient

Otherwise they just compare you to a clown with face paint.

# 5. Pricing recommendation summary

## Best initial posted “starting from” prices
- Mini Deco: **from €240**
- Mini Play: **from €320**
- Signature Plend: **from €590**
- Premium Plend: **from €950**

These are good starting anchors.
Actual quotes can flex upward based on:
- distance
- event size
- duration
- custom theme complexity
- staffing required

---

# 6. Final take
The benchmark supports this conclusion:

**Plend should not enter as a budget provider.**

The smarter move is:
- premium but not absurd
- clear package ladder
- strong visuals
- quote flexibility for bigger setups

That gives you room to survive the logistics and still look worth paying for.
