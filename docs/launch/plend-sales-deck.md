# Plend Sales Deck Draft

## Slide 1. Cover
**Plend**
Beautiful birthday moments for little ones

Subline:
Safe play, beautiful parties, zero stress.

---

## Slide 2. The problem
Planning a birthday party for a child under 6 is more work than it looks.

Parents often need to coordinate:
- decoration
- entertainment
- setup
- supervision
- cleanup

The result is often stressful, generic, or both.

---

## Slide 3. The solution
Plend creates fully managed birthday setups for young children.

We combine:
- themed decoration
- toddler-friendly game stations
- delivery and setup
- on-site supervision
- teardown and pickup

Parents enjoy the day. We handle the scene.

---

## Slide 4. Initial market
Starting zones:
- Gavà Mar
- Castelldefels

Why here:
- family households
- strong fit for home and private-space celebrations
- good local density for repeatable logistics
- customer base more likely to value convenience and aesthetics

---

## Slide 5. Who it is for
Primary customer:
- parents of children under 6
- families who care about beautiful celebrations
- customers who want less stress and more convenience

Not for:
- ultra-budget party buyers
- generic entertainer-only expectations

---

## Slide 6. What makes Plend different
- under-6 specialization
- decor + play + supervision in one service
- premium home-party positioning
- stylish and photo-ready setups
- local, hands-on execution

This is not a generic animation company.
It is a boutique children’s party service.

Suggested starting prices:

- Mini Play: from **€320**

- Premium Plend: from **€950**

## Slide 9. Market and competition
We compete against:
- indoor birthday venues
- at-home animators
- decor-only providers
- DIY parties

Plend wins by offering:
- better aesthetics than generic party services
- more convenience than DIY
- more intimacy and personalization than a venue
- more toddler focus than broad entertainment companies

---

## Slide 10. Business model
Revenue comes from:
- package sales
- premium upgrades
- add-ons
- extra supervision hours
- additional decor elements

The business works best when:
- average ticket is protected
- setup modules are reusable
- operations stay tight and repeatable

---

## Slide 11. Financial snapshot
Estimated startup range:
- **€9,000 to €13,500** for a solid launch

Monthly break-even direction:
- around **3 bookings/month** to cover fixed monthly costs
- around **4 to 6 bookings/month** to start feeling commercially healthy

Base-case revenue path:
- **€2,000 to €3,900/month** in early traction

## Slide 12. Go-to-market
Launch with:
- 2 to 3 reusable themes
- 1 to 2 play station formats
- Instagram-first visual marketing
- local partnerships and referrals
- strong photo/video content

Primary channels:
- Instagram

- schools and childcare referrals
