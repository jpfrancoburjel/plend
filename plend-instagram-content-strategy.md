# Plend Instagram and Content Strategy

## Goal
Use Instagram as the primary early demand engine for Plend in Gavà Mar and Castelldefels.

Why Instagram first:
- this business is visual
- mothers and families in the target segment browse for inspiration visually
- social proof matters a lot
- local word of mouth compounds well when the setup photographs beautifully

---

# 1. Core strategy

## Main idea
Plend should not post like a generic event page.
It should post like a **boutique kids celebration brand**.

The feed should make people think:
- this is beautiful
- this feels easy
- this is safe for little kids
- I want this at my child’s birthday

---

# 2. Content pillars

## Pillar 1: Beautiful setups
Post:
- full event reveals
- before/after setup shots
- close-up decor details
- styled cake table moments
- soft play zone hero shots

## Pillar 2: Kids experience
Post:
- children enjoying the space, with permission
- toddler-safe play interaction moments
- calm happy scenes, not visual chaos

## Pillar 3: Parent relief / convenience
Post:
- “we handle setup and pickup” messages
- behind-the-scenes prep
- loading / setup flow
- how easy the booking process is

## Pillar 4: Themes and packages
Post:
- theme spotlights
- package comparisons
- what each package includes
- add-on ideas

## Pillar 5: Trust
Post:
- testimonials
- process explanations
- safety/hygiene standards
- professionalism, punctuality, care

---

# 3. Best content formats

## Reels
Priority format.
Use for:
- setup timelapses
- transformation videos
- theme reveals
- before/after scenes

## Carousel posts
Use for:
- package breakdowns
- event storytelling
- “3 reasons parents love this package” style content

## Stories
Use for:
- daily activity
- behind the scenes
- availability
- polls
- quick social proof

## Highlights
Create highlights for:
- Packages
- Themes
- Soft Play
- Reviews
- FAQs
- Gavà Mar
- Castelldefels

---

# 4. Posting plan

## Launch phase
- **3 feed posts per week**
- **3 to 6 stories per week**
- **1 to 2 reels per week**

## Content mix example
- Monday: carousel, package or theme
- Wednesday: reel, setup or transformation
- Friday: client result / styled reveal
- weekend stories: behind the scenes or live event fragments

# 6. Local strategy

## Location emphasis
Always reinforce:
- Gavà Mar
- Castelldefels
- nearby areas only when relevant

## Why
Local trust matters. People need to feel:
- you really operate here
- you understand the area
- you are accessible and reliable

## Useful captions / CTA patterns
- “Celebrating in Gavà Mar?”
- “Looking for a beautiful under-6 birthday setup in Castelldefels?”
- “DM us to reserve your date”

---

# 7. Visual rules

## The feed should feel
- clean
- soft
- premium
- warm
- not cluttered

## Visual mistakes to avoid
- noisy mixed branding
- too many fonts/colors
- low-light phone photos
- generic stock-looking posts
- cheap promo graphics

---

# 8. Conversion path

## Instagram should drive toward
- DM inquiry
- WhatsApp inquiry
- simple quote form

## Suggested CTA system
- “Send us your date and child’s age”
- “DM us your party theme idea”
- “Ask for our package guide”

Keep it easy and low friction.
