# Plend Launch Package Catalog

## Purpose
Define a clean initial package menu for Plend.

The goal is not to offer everything.
The goal is to launch with offers that are:
- easy to understand
- easy to sell
- operationally repeatable
- premium enough to protect margin

---

# 1. Package strategy

## Rules for launch
- Keep only **3 to 4 core packages**
- Build them from reusable modules
- Avoid too many custom one-off themes at the start
- Make upgrade paths obvious

## Best launch logic
Start with:
1. decor-only package
2. play-only package
3. full package
4. premium package

That is enough.

---

# 2. Package 1: Mini Deco

## Best for
- small birthdays
- families who already have activities covered
- apartment or terrace events

## Includes
- mini balloon setup
- themed backdrop or decor wall
- cake table styling
- basic personalized sign
- setup and pickup

## Recommended price band
- **€180 to €300**

## Notes
This should be visually clean, but not too labor-heavy.
Good lead generator, but not the business hero.

---

# 3. Package 2: Mini Play

## Best for
- toddlers and preschoolers
- small home parties
- parents who mainly want safe entertainment structure

## Includes
- one compact soft play zone
- mats
- ball pit or one main attraction
- toddler-safe ride-on or activity unit
- setup and pickup

## Recommended price band
- **€250 to €450**

## Notes
This is useful, but the real margin gets better when combined with decor.

---

# 4. Package 3: Signature Plend

## Best for
- the core business
- parents who want the whole thing handled
- under-6 home birthday celebrations

## Includes
- themed decor setup
- one soft play / game station area
- personalized sign
- setup
- on-site supervision during agreed time block
- teardown and pickup

## Recommended price band
- **€450 to €800**

## Notes
This should become the bestseller.
If this package is not attractive, the business positioning is off.

---

# 5. Package 4: Premium Plend

## Best for
- larger birthdays
- more style-conscious families
- premium homes / gardens / private spaces

## Includes
- bigger themed install
- expanded play area
- more personalized decor elements
- premium sign / name feature
- longer supervision block
- more refined styling details
- setup, teardown, pickup

## Recommended price band
- **€850 to €1,500+**

## Notes
This is the aspiration package.
You do not need many of these, but they help anchor the brand upward.

---

# 6. Add-ons

## Safe add-ons for launch
- extra supervision hour
- extra balloon set
- additional personalized signage
- second play module
- party favor styling
- custom welcome sign
- themed character visual element

## Add-ons to delay
- too many live entertainers
- highly custom handmade prop builds
- big one-off structures
- lots of themes with unique inventory

Delay those until demand is real.

---

# 7. Theme recommendations for launch

## Start with 3 reusable themes
### A. Soft Pastel / Luxury Kids
- broad appeal
- works for many ages under 6
- premium photos

### B. Safari / Jungle
- timeless
- gender-flexible
- easy to style repeatedly

### C. Princess / Fairytale or Vehicles / Construction
Pick one based on demand and asset cost.

## Why only 3 themes
Because too many themes at launch create:
- storage mess
- higher upfront spend
- slower setup
- weaker brand cohesion

---

# 8. Package design rules

## Every package should answer 3 questions fast
1. What does it include?
2. Who is it for?
3. Why is it worth the price?

## Sales language should emphasize
- convenience
- beauty
- safe play for young kids
- stress reduction for parents

Do not write like a random entertainer site.
Write like a boutique service brand.

---

# 9. Suggested catalog layout for web or PDF

## Intro section
- what Plend is
- target age
- service zones

## Then the 4 packages
For each package show:
- package name
- hero photo
- 4 to 6 included bullets
- ideal event type
- “starting from” price

## Then add-ons
Simple, not bloated.

## Then booking process
- inquiry
- quote
- confirm date
- Plend handles the rest

---

# 10. Recommendation
For launch, push hardest on:
- **Signature Plend**
- **Premium Plend**

Use Mini Deco and Mini Play mainly as:
- entry offers
- lead generators
- upsell steps

That gives you a cleaner business than surviving on low-ticket jobs.
